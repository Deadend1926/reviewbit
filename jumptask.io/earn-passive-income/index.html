How does passive income work?

What is passive income?

Passive income is money you earn with little to no ongoing effort after getting started. Instead of working hourly or for a fixed salary, you build an income stream that continues to generate earnings over time.

Some passive income strategies require an initial investment, like purchasing rental properties or investing in dividend-paying stocks. Others, such as affiliate marketing or money market accounts, let you earn without heavy upfront costs. The best passive income ideas work in the background, allowing you to reach your financial goals with minimal effort.

Common ways to earn passive income

Many people start making passive income by investing in money market accounts, savings accounts, or stock dividends.
