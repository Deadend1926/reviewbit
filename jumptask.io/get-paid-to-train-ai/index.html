What is AI training?

AI training is the process of teaching artificial intelligence systems to perform smarter and more accurate tasks using human-labeled data. Activities like labeling images, annotating text, and categorizing information are essential in helping AI models improve and adapt to real-world scenarios.

Humans play a crucial role in shaping the future of artificial intelligence by participating in these activities. Platforms like JumpTask make it easy to find such tasks, offering opportunities for anyone to contribute while earning money along the way.
