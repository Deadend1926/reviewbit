What are micro jobs?

Micro jobs are small, simple online tasks that anyone can complete to earn extra money. Unlike a regular job, they don’t require long-term commitments or special skills. You could take surveys, play games, test apps, tag images, or engage on social media. Just choose a task, finish it, and watch your earnings grow.
