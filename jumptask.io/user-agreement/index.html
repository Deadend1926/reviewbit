4. BALANCE AND PAYOUTS

JMPT Balance. The amount earnings in your Platform account will be displayed within the Platform in their equivalent value of JMPTs at all times.
This does not mean that you already own the displayed amount of JMPTs at that particular time. Before you request a Payout (by clicking on the “Transfer to My Wallet” button within the Platform), you only have the right to the value of your earnings, which are displayed in their equivalent value in JMPTs within the Platform. Once you request a Payout, we will transfer the amount JMPTs equal to its value of earnings within your Platform account of the particular Payout transaction.
​Invitations. You may invite third parties to use Jumptask and accumulate additional JMPT’s by receiving a percentage of such invited third party’s collected balance. Any additional amounts due to invitations shall be displayed on the Jumptask Platform. All terms about additional accumulations due to third party invitations can be found on the Jumptask Platform.
Payout. You will be able request a Payout up to the value of your earnings within the Platform by clicking on the “Transfer to My Wallet” button within the Platform. The JMPTs will then be transferred to the crypto wallet connected to your account in JMPTs.
Gas Fee. Platform users are required to pay a block-chain transaction fee for every requested Payout (“Gas Fee”).
The size of the Gas Fee will be deducted from the Payout you receive every time you request it and will be displayed to you at all times when requesting a Payout within the Platform.
Please note that even though there are no general limitations to the minimum value of earnings that you may need to have in your Platform account to request a Payout, the value of any such transaction may never be lower than the Gas Fee.
Gas Fee Rate.  The size of the Gas Fee displayed at the moment you request a Payout may slightly differ from its size that was actually deducted from the Payout you receive. This is because the size of the Gas Fee, like most other block-chain transaction fees, is variable and constantly changing.
While you will always be able to see the current rate of the Gas Fee within the Platform, the processing of any Payout request may take enough time for the size of the Gas Fee to change. This means that after you request a Payout, you may receive a little more or less than what was displayed as the current value of the JMPT at the moment of making the Payout request. While it’s not likely that the size of the Gas Fee will vary drastically from the moment you request a Payout to the moment the JMPTs are actually transferred to your crypto wallet, by using the Platform you represent full understanding and acceptance of this possibility. In no situation will JumpTask be required to pay you any such differences or be responsible for any losses suffered in using the Platform.
