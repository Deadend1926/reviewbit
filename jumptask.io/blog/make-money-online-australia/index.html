2. Participate in market research and user testing

Market research is just a fancy term for sharing your opinion on products, services, or websites. Research methods can include surveys, focus groups, or testing apps before they launch. Compared to simple tasks, these pay more per session but appear less often.
User testing usually requires clear feedback and basic computer skills, but nothing too advanced. 
If you like sharing opinions and testing new products, this option can fit easily into your schedule. To get started, you can sign up on the following platforms:
  • UserTesting: Reviewing websites or apps and earning around $10 for a 20-minute session, with payments typically sent to PayPal within a week.
  • Userlytics: Taking part in live or self-guided tests for new products. Once you complete a session, your earnings are paid out via PayPal.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: A few days
Ease of use: Easy

3. Do data entry and basic online admin work

Data entry typically includes updating spreadsheets, entering customer details, or sorting information. If you are detail-oriented and comfortable following instructions, this can be a steady way to make money as a woman.
Many roles are flexible and allow you to work at your own schedule, which makes them perfect if you are juggling a full-time job or have major family commitments.
You can find this type of work on platforms like:
  • Freelancer.com.au: Here, you can apply for data entry or admin jobs that pay per project or hourly, with payments handled securely through the platform.
  • Truelancer: On this platform, you complete clearly defined tasks and receive payment once your work is approved.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: Days
Ease of use: Easy

4. Become a virtual assistant

A virtual assistant handles simple tasks for small business owners. Entry-level work can include tasks such as inbox sorting, calendar updates, basic social media management activities, or responding to customer inquiries.
Many beginners start with one client and gradually grow as their confidence builds. These tasks are great for gaining experience in business and marketing strategy that can be very useful if your ultimate goal is to run your own business in the future.
You can start by looking for entry-level roles on platforms like these:
  • Upwork: You can apply for VA jobs such as scheduling or email management, and your payment is held in escrow until the work is completed.
  • Freelancer.com.au: On this platform, you can take on smaller VA tasks with milestone payments, which helps you build confidence without long commitments.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: 1 to 2 weeks
Ease of use: Medium

5. Freelance simple skills online

Freelancing does not always mean advanced design or web development. Many beginners offer basic services like proofreading, formatting documents, simple graphic design, or updating marketing materials.
The key is to start with services you already feel comfortable offering. Over time, your skills will improve naturally as you gain more clients.
You can list your services on these platforms designed for beginners:
  • Fiverr: You can create a service listing for things like proofreading or formatting, and clients come to you.
  • Upwork: Here, you can send proposals for jobs that match your skill level and decide which projects you want to take on, and you can set your own rates.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: 1 to 3 weeks
Ease of use: Medium

6. Sell unused items online

If you need extra cash fast, selling unused items is one of the easiest ways to make money online. You can sell clothes, electronics, books, furniture, or any other items you don’t need or use anymore by listing them on online marketplaces.
You can not earn passive income long-term, but it is an effective method for quick wins and clearing space at home.
If you want to make money as a man quickly without learning anything new, selling items you no longer use is one of the simplest options. You can list items on platforms people already browse daily, such as:
  • Gumtree: You can post local listings for items like furniture or electronics and often arrange in-person pickup.
  • Facebook Marketplace: On Facebook Marketplace, you can reach nearby buyers easily and sell through local pickup or shipping.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: Same week
Ease of use: Easy

7. Turn handmade products into a small business online

If you already enjoy crafts, baking, or creating personalised items, you can make and sell simple items like candles, prints, or custom gifts. These product types work best when you are just starting, but don’t expect to turn them into a money-making machine right away.
Keep expectations realistic and start small to avoid unnecessary costs.
Explore these platforms for selling products and managing orders in one place:
  • Etsy: Sell handmade or custom items using built-in tools for payments, shipping, and customer messages.
  • Madeit.com.au: List products in a marketplace focused on locally made goods, which attracts buyers looking for handmade items.
Beginner-friendly: Medium
How fast you can earn: Weeks
Ease of use: Medium

8. Sell digital products online

Templates, planners, checklists, or short guides are some of the most sought-after digital products, and once you create them, you can sell them over and over again. These require upfront effort but no physical delivery. 
This approach could be the best fit for you if you enjoy personal projects and want more control over your business model.
These are some of the platforms you can use to sell digital products online:
  • Gumroad: Upload your file, set a price, and the platform delivers it instantly after purchase.
  • Etsy: You can also sell digital downloads here, which works well if you already have creative files ready.
Beginner-friendly: Medium
How fast you can earn: Weeks
Ease of use: Medium

9. Teach or tutor online

Online tutoring doesn’t necessarily require you to have a formal education. English conversation, basic maths, or beginner software skills are also in demand. You do not need teaching qualifications to teach online courses for most platforms.
If you enjoy explaining things and working with people, online tutoring can become a reliable income stream. 
You can connect with students through the following platforms:
  • Preply: Set your availability and teach scheduled lessons or online courses in subjects you are comfortable with.
  • iTalki: If you prefer informal teaching, this platform allows you to schedule conversational sessions.
Beginner-friendly: Medium
How fast you can earn: 1 to 2 weeks
Ease of use: Medium

10. Moderate content or communities online

Content moderation is about reviewing posts, comments, or listings with the goal of making sure all guidelines are followed consistently. The work is structured and usually paid hourly.
You get clear instructions to follow, so the work does not require you to provide creative input or have advanced digital marketing knowledge.
You can find this type of work on the following platforms:
  • Clickworker: Reviewing content according to set rules and receiving regular PayPal payouts once tasks are completed.
  • Appen: Working on longer projects that follow consistent workflows and instructions.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: Days to weeks
Ease of use: Easy

11. Try cashback and reward apps

Cashback apps reward you for your everyday spending. While this will not replace a full-time job in any way, shape, or form, it helps generate extra money for purchases you already make in online stores.
Think of this as a way to recover some costs rather than generate a passive income stream.
If you already shop online, cashback apps help you earn small amounts without extra effort. You can start by exploring the following cashback apps:
  • ShopBack: Earn cashback on purchases and withdraw your earnings once they add up.
  • Cashrewards: Get a percentage back on everyday shopping, paid directly to your account.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: Weeks
Ease of use: Very easy

12. Transcribe audio or captions online

Transcription is simply converting audio into text. Audio files can be anything from interviews, captions, to legal transcription. Don’t worry if you are a slow typer, as speed will improve with practice. You only need to make sure your output is accurate, and the rest will fall into place in time.
If you like focused, independent work, transcription can be a good option. You listen to audio and convert it into text on platforms like these:
  • Rev.com: You choose transcription or caption tasks and get paid weekly through PayPal.
  • TranscribeMe: You start with short audio clips, which helps you build speed and accuracy over time.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: Days
Ease of use: Medium

13. Create content and earn from views or links

You can create content by writing blogs, making short videos, or running a YouTube channel focused on practical topics. Popular profitable niches often relate to personal finance, tutorials, or everyday problem-solving.
Results take time, but this can become a reliable way to generate income long term.
If you are willing to be patient and consistent, content creation can pay off in time. You can monetise your work through platforms like:
  • YouTube Partner Program: Earn from ads once your channel meets the eligibility requirements.
  • Medium Partner Program: Get paid based on how much time members spend reading your articles.
Beginner-friendly: Medium
How fast you can earn: Months
Ease of use: Medium

14. Affiliate marketing for beginners

Affiliate marketing works like this: you recommend a product or service using your own special link, and when someone clicks that link and ends up buying something, you earn a small commission for the referral. 
You can start by sharing links in articles, guides, or simple social media posts.
You are unlikely to make money fast, but you can grow it into a strong passive income option if you stay consistent.
You can start with programs that are easy to join:
  • Amazon Associates AU: Share product links and earn a commission when someone makes a purchase.
  • Commission Factory: Access affiliate offers from local brands and track performance in one dashboard.
Beginner-friendly: Medium
How fast you can earn: Months
Ease of use: Medium

15. Offer local services booked online for more money

Some services blend online and offline work. These can include cleaning, tutoring, various physical work, and more.
If you want higher hourly pay and flexible scheduling, offering local services can be a strong option. You can get booked through platforms like:
  • Airtasker: You accept local jobs such as cleaning or odd jobs and get paid after completing the task.
  • Hipages – You receive service requests from people looking for ongoing or one-off help.
Beginner-friendly: Yes
How fast you can earn: Days
Ease of use: Easy

Common mistakes when trying to earn extra cash online

Once you decide to start making money online, it’s important to know and remember the following:
  • There will be no instant results without effort
  • Never pay upfront fees to unknown platforms
  • Don’t ignore tax implications
  • Try not to engage in too many options at once
