Introducing $JMPT Cross-Chain Bridges: Polygon and Celo Networks

Gintare Rimolaityte
Updated on December 5, 2025
Share:
JumpTask is excited to announce that $JMPT is now available on Polygon and Celo. Previously, $JMPT was only on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Starting in April 2023, token holders can easily move $JMPT between BSC, Polygon, and Celo. 
This expansion increases the token’s reach, making it more accessible and valuable for a broader audience.

Why cross-chain bridges are important

Cross-chain bridges play a crucial role in the ever-evolving world of cryptocurrency. They allow tokens to move between different blockchain networks, an essential function as decentralized finance (DeFi) and other blockchain applications continue to diversify. 
By removing barriers between networks and users, cross-chain bridges help projects expand their reach, attract a larger user base, and increase overall liquidity.
The expansion of $JMPT to Polygon and Celo is a testament to JumpTask’s commitment to embracing new technologies and ensuring its ecosystem remains adaptable and forward-thinking. 
These newly established bridges not only improve the accessibility of $JMPT but also promote the ongoing collaboration and interoperability between various blockchain communities.

Key advantages of Polygon and Celo

Expanding $JMPT to multiple blockchains brings significant benefits, especially with networks like Polygon and Celo. Each offers unique strengths that enhance accessibility, speed, and cost efficiency for token holders.

Polygon Network

As one of the most popular Ethereum scaling solutions, Polygon is known for its fast transactions and low fees. It provides a developer-friendly ecosystem and seamless compatibility with Ethereum-based applications, making it an excellent choice for expanding $JMPT’s usability.
Polygon Network Contract Address: 0x03cf5d93ca7c70ce0a21a09f4d70779d2c66b25a

Celo Network

Celo stands out for its mobile-first approach, focusing on delivering financial tools and services to mobile users worldwide. By bridging to Celo, $JMPT becomes more accessible to a global audience, reinforcing JumpTask’s vision of inclusive and borderless financial solutions.
Celo Network Contract Address: 0x1d18d0386f51ab03e7e84e71bda1681eba865f1f

Simplifying transfers with PortalBridge

To ensure a smooth experience when moving $JMPT across different networks, JumpTask recommends using PortalBridge. This service allows you to transfer tokens between the BSC, Polygon, and Celo chains securely and with minimal effort. 
The straightforward interface guides users through the bridging process step by step, making it accessible to both newcomers and veteran blockchain enthusiasts alike.

Opportunities for growth and innovation

This cross-chain bridging initiative is more than just a convenient feature. It’s a major milestone for the JumpTask ecosystem. By connecting with two of the most promising blockchain networks, JumpTask aims to:
  • Broaden its audience by reaching new communities of traders, developers, and investors.
  • Expand its ecosystem through partnerships and integrations with DeFi platforms, NFT marketplaces, and other decentralized applications (dApps) across multiple chains.
  • Drive innovation in the JumpTask project by encouraging the development of new features, products, and incentives that enhance user engagement.
With these bridges in place, $JMPT is poised to become even more versatile and valuable to token holders, furthering JumpTask’s mission to bring more inclusive financial opportunities to the blockchain space.

Looking ahead

As the JumpTask team continues to evolve and strengthen its ecosystem, cross-chain functionality will remain at the heart of its growth strategy. The integration with Polygon and Celo marks the beginning of an exciting journey toward greater interoperability and user-centric design.
Stay tuned for further enhancements and updates as JumpTask builds upon this foundation and introduces more cutting-edge features, collaborations, and improvements in the months to come. By investing in innovative technologies and strengthening partnerships, JumpTask remains dedicated to unlocking the full potential of blockchain and DeFi for its global community.
