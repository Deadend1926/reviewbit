The crypto industry evolves quickly, and staying informed is key. Podcasts deliver expert insights, real-time analysis, and deep dives into Bitcoin, DeFi, and market trends. This curated list highlights the best options, from investigative reporting to beginner-friendly discussions.

Comparing the top crypto podcasts: a shortlist 

With a wide range of crypto podcasts available, this breakdown helps you quickly find one that matches your interests and experience level.
No matter your experience level, there’s a cryptocurrency podcast that fits your needs. Next, we’ll explore these options in more detail to help you choose the best one.

1. Unchained

  • Host: Laura Shin (crypto journalist, former Forbes editor)
  • Best for: People who want investigative reporting and exclusive industry insights
Unchained sets itself apart with deep investigative journalism on crypto. Hosted by Laura Shin, a respected journalist, it features exclusive interviews with industry leaders, investors, and policymakers.
Key episodes have uncovered major crypto events, including the true identity of the DAO hacker and deep dives into the FTX collapse, SEC lawsuits, and Ethereum’s transition to proof-of-stake. 
It’s a must-listen for those who want fact-based analysis beyond speculation.
Why listen? If you’re looking for expert-driven insights and investigative stories that shape the crypto industry, Unchained is one of the best sources.
Listen to Unchained on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

2. Bankless

  • Hosts: Ryan Sean Adams (@RyanSAdams) & David Hoffman (@TrustlessState)
    Best for: Ethusiasts who want to understand DeFi, Ethereum, and financial self-sovereignty
Bankless is a leading cryptocurrency podcast focused on decentralized finance and Ethereum. Hosted by Ryan Sean Adams and David Hoffman, it helps people transition from beginners to active participants in the crypto economy.
Key episodes explore Ethereum’s role in DeFi, staking mechanics, and feature industry stars like Vitalik Buterin and Balaji Srinivasan. 
Covering DeFi strategies and crypto market trends, it’s one of the best crypto podcasts on Spotify for those managing digital assets.
Why listen? If you want a structured approach to financial independence in crypto, Bankless delivers expert insights and practical strategies.
Listen to Bankless on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

3. The Pomp Podcast

  • Host: Anthony Pompliano (@APompliano)
  • Best for: People interested in Bitcoin, finance, and the broader crypto market
The Pomp Podcast, hosted by investor Anthony Pompliano, covers Bitcoin, finance, and macroeconomics. It blends expert insights with real-world applications, making it valuable for both beginners and experienced investors.
Key episodes feature speakers like Michael Saylor and Chamath Palihapitiya, offering clear insights on Bitcoin news, market trends, and financial innovation. 
Pomp’s engaging interview style makes complex topics accessible.
Why listen? If you want expert perspectives on Bitcoin’s role in global finance, this podcast delivers actionable insights in a digestible format.
Listen to The Pomp Podcast on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

4. What Bitcoin Did

  • Host: Peter McCormack (@PeterMcCormack)
    Best for: Listeners who want deep insights into Bitcoin’s technology, adoption, and regulation
What Bitcoin Did is the go-to podcast for Bitcoin maximalists. Hosted by Peter McCormack, it features in-depth conversations with Bitcoin developers, economists, and investors, offering a no-nonsense take on Bitcoin’s role in global finance.
Key episodes explore Bitcoin adoption, regulatory battles, and the technology behind it, with guests like Adam Back and Lyn Alden. 
McCormack’s straightforward approach makes complex topics digestible, covering monetary policy, blockchain technology, and energy consumption debates. As one of the best cryptocurrency podcasts, it’s a top choice for those serious about Bitcoin.
Why listen? If you want an in-depth, Bitcoin-only podcast that explores everything from tech to regulation, this is the best choice.
Listen to What Bitcoin Did on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

5. The Bad Crypto Podcast

  • Hosts: Joel Comm (@joelcomm) & Travis Wright (@teedubya)
    Best for: Cryptocurrency beginners looking for fun, easy-to-understand discussions
The Bad Crypto Podcast makes learning about blockchain technology and crypto investing entertaining. Hosted by Joel Comm and Travis Wright, it simplifies complex crypto topics with humor and engaging conversations.
Key episodes cover blockchain fundamentals, market trends, and emerging technologies, breaking down concepts in a way anyone can understand. 
With a casual tone and lighthearted banter, it’s the Bad Crypto Podcast of the best cryptocurrency podcasts on Spotify for newcomers exploring the cryptocurrency world.
Why listen? If you want a beginner-friendly podcast that makes crypto fun and easy to grasp, this is a great pick.
Listen to The Bad Crypto Podcast on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

6. Epicenter

  • Hosts: Sébastien Couture (@seb3point0), Friederike Ernst, Meher Roy (@MeherRoy), Brian Fabian Crain (@crainbf)
  • Best for: People interested in blockchain technology, in-depth technical discussions, and entrepreneurship in crypto
Epicenter is a leading cryptocurrency podcast that explores the technology and economics behind blockchain. Hosted by a panel of industry experts, it features deep-dive discussions with developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs shaping the crypto space.
Key episodes cover blockchain scalability, governance models, and the latest advancements in DeFi. With guests like Gavin Wood and Anatoly Yakovenko, Epicenter provides expert insights on the cryptocurrency world and its future innovations. 
As a weekly podcast, it offers regular updates on emerging trends.
Why listen? If you want a highly technical yet accessible podcast that covers blockchain innovation, Epicenter delivers expert-level discussions with industry leaders.
Listen to Epicenter on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

7. The Defiant Podcast

  • Host: Camila Russo (@CamiRusso)
  • Best for: Listeners who want DeFi insights, cryptocurrency news, and startup perspectives
The Defiant Podcast is one of the best crypto news podcast, offering in-depth discussions on DeFi, Web3, and blockchain startups. Hosted by journalist and entrepreneur Camila Russo, it features interviews with DeFi co-founders, investors, and developers building the decentralized economy.
Key episodes analyze major DeFi projects, smart contract security, and market disruptions. Guests like Stani Kulechov and Rune Christensen provide first-hand insights into decentralized finance and crypto entrepreneurship.
As a weekly podcast, it keeps people informed on the latest industry shifts.
Why listen? If you want expert breakdowns of DeFi and blockchain startups, The Defiant Podcast is a must-listen for anyone following the decentralized economy.
Listen to The Defiant Podcast on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

8. The Wolf of All Streets

  • Host: Scott Melker (@scottmelker)
    Best for: Listeners interested in crypto investing, market analysis, and trading strategies
The Wolf of All Streets offers a deep dive into crypto markets, trading strategies, and economic trends. Hosted by trader and analyst Scott Melker, it explores how digital assets are reshaping global finance and how they compare to traditional financial institutions.
Key episodes feature insights from investors, fund managers, and entrepreneurs like Raoul Pal and Mike Novogratz. 
Melker breaks down price movements, risk management strategies, and emerging trends, making complex market dynamics easier to understand.
Why listen? If you’re looking for expert perspectives on cryptocurrency investments and market trends, this podcast provides actionable insights from seasoned traders and industry professionals.
Listen to The Wolf of All Streets on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

9. Crypto 101

  • Hosts: Bryce Paul (@BrycePaul101) & Aaron Malone
  • Best for: Beginners looking for an easy introduction to blockchain and crypto assets
Crypto 101 is one of the most beginner-friendly podcast episodes for those new to blockchain. Hosted by Bryce Paul and Aaron Malone, it simplifies crypto concepts and breaks down the basics in a way that’s easy to understand.
The show covers essential topics like wallets, security, and emerging crypto trends. The hosts also introduce listeners to other podcasts and resources that help build a strong foundation in the crypto space.
Why listen? If you’re just starting out in crypto and want clear, easy-to-follow explanations, Crypto 101 is the perfect learning resource.
Listen to Crypto 101 on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

10. The a16z Podcast

  • Hosts: Andreessen Horowitz team (@a16z)
  • Best for: People interested in the intersection of crypto, technology, and venture capital
The a16z Podcast, produced by venture capital firm Andreessen Horowitz, explores blockchain, Web3, and emerging tech trends. It provides insights from leading investors, entrepreneurs, and developers shaping the future of decentralized technologies.
Key podcast episodes feature discussions on DeFi, token economies, and blockchain adoption, with guests like Vitalik Buterin and Chris Dixon. It also highlights other podcasts and resources for deeper industry knowledge.
Why listen? If you want high-level insights on blockchain's role in tech innovation, this podcast offers expert perspectives from industry leaders.
Listen to The a16z Podcast on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

11. CoinDesk Podcasts

  • Hosts: CoinDesk editorial team (@CoinDesk)
  • Best for: People who want daily reports and expert insight
CoinDesk Podcasts is a crypto podcast giving the latest industry news, covering market trends, regulations, and industry developments. Produced by one of the most trusted crypto media platforms, it offers multiple shows like "Markets Daily" and "The Breakdown."
Key podcast episodes analyze price movements, regulatory updates, and macroeconomic factors influencing crypto. 
The editorial team frequently features industry experts and recommends other podcasts for deeper insights.
Why listen? If you need timely updates from a reliable source, CoinDesk Podcasts provide in-depth reporting and expert opinions.
Listen to CoinDesk Podcasts on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.

12. Thinking Crypto News & Interviews

  • Host: Tony Edward (@ThinkingCrypto1)
  • Best for: People looking for expert interviews, regulatory updates, and news
Thinking Crypto News & Interviews is a cryptocurrency podcast that covers daily news, industry developments, and in-depth conversations with key figures in crypto. Hosted by Tony Edward, it features a mix of market updates and exclusive interviews with investors, project founders, and policymakers.
Key podcast episodes include discussions with SEC commissioners, Ripple executives, and blockchain leaders, offering insights into regulatory shifts and emerging trends. 
The podcast also highlights other podcasts and resources to help listeners stay updated.
Why listen? If you want a balanced mix of news and expert opinions on crypto industry developments, this podcast provides valuable insights from insiders shaping the space.
Listen to Thinking Crypto News & Interviews on Spotify or Apple Podcasts.
