JumpTask Black Friday Giveaway – Official Rules

Gabriele Zundaite
Updated on December 5, 2025
Share:
This year’s Black Friday campaign comes with something extra. Alongside boosted rewards and seasonal tasks, we’re introducing a special giveaway for Jumptaskers who stay the most active throughout the event.
This guide walks you through everything you need to know about the Giveaway and outlines all Official Rules and Conditions. Feel free to bookmark it and check back anytime during the campaign.
The JumpTask Black Friday Giveaway (“Giveaway”) is organized by JumpTask. By participating, you agree to these Official Rules.

Join the Black Friday campaign

Boosted rewards, Happy Hours, and seasonal tasks are already live. Jump in!

About the Giveaway

The Black Friday Giveaway is part of our Black Friday, Non-Stop 2025 campaign. Once you complete all required campaign offers, you’ll be automatically entered into the prize draw at the end of the event.
There’s nothing extra you need to fill out or pay for. Just make sure you stay active and enjoy the event.
Here’s a quick reminder of the main Black Friday campaign rules, which also apply to the Giveaway:
  • Start of the event: November 17th. 
  • Last day of the event: December 8th. 
  • Double rewards across selected tasks
  • Triple rewards during surprise Happy Hours
  • Exclusive prizes for winners
  • Giveaway winners announced 1–2 weeks after the campaign ends

Who can participate in the Black Friday Giveaway?

Everyone is welcome. All registered JumpTask users with an active account can join the Giveaway, no matter where they’re located: 
  • No additional sign up
  • No purchase required
If you’re completing tasks on the platform, you can take part.

How to enter

During the Black Friday campaign, you’ll see a list of offers on the official Black Friday page inside the platform. 
To enter the Giveaway, you must complete all offers shown on that list before the event ends in December 2025.
A few very important notes:
  • You must complete the entire list of offers to qualify
  • Even leaving 1–2 offers unfinished means you’re not eligible
  • New offers may appear every other day
  • Checking the page regularly increases your chances of completing everything in time
Only once you finish the full list, your entry is confirmed automatically.

Prizes you can win

This year’s Giveaway includes a mix of physical items and JMPT rewards. 
All Jumptaskers are competing for: 
  • 1x PlayStation 5 (approx. value $500)
  • 5x JBL Go 3 speakers (approx. total value $225)
  • 30x 10 JMPT tokens (approx. total value $300)
All prizes will be distributed after the campaign ends (December 8th).

Prize options for physical items

If you win a physical prize, you’ll be able to choose between two options:
1. Get the physical item. We’ll send it to the address you provide. 
Note: JumpTask is not responsible for any loss or damage occurring during shipment or after dispatch.
2. Take the prize value in JMPT. You can choose to receive the official retail value of the prize as JMPT tokens. The conversion will use the JMPT rate on the day the prize is paid out.

How winners of the official Giveaway are selected

After the Black Friday campaign ends, winners will be randomly selected from all users who completed every listed offer.
Here’s what happens next:
  • Winners are selected randomly
  • The draw takes place within 1–2 weeks after the campaign ends
  • Each winner will be contacted via email 
  • Results will also be shared through our usual communication channels
If your JumpTask account isn’t connected to an email, you’ll need to reach out to [email protected] to claim your prize.
If selected winners don’t reply within 15 business days after the announcement, their physical prizes will be converted into their JMPT equivalents and automatically added to their JumpTask accounts.

Final notes

This Giveaway is meant to bring a little extra excitement to the Black Friday season for our most active users, and we want to ensure a fair experience for everyone.
To make this clear:
  • All decisions made by JumpTask are final.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for other rewards unless specifically stated in these rules.
  • JumpTask reserves the right to modify, suspend, or cancel the Giveaway if necessary due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • Participation in the Giveaway implies acceptance of these Official Rules, as well as JumpTask’s general Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
If anything changes or updates are needed, we’ll share information directly through:
  • The JumpTask app
  • Our official blog
  • Our official X and Discord channels
Stay active, keep an eye on new offers, and good luck!
