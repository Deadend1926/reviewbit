You can get paid to answer questions online by using platforms that value your insights and expertise. There are options for all skill levels, from simple opinions to in-depth explanations. If you're looking for a flexible job for quiet personalities, this might be your ideal side gig.
Let’s dive in!

1. JumpTask

JumpTask is a great platform to start with if you want to get paid to answer questions without jumping through hoops. It offers a variety of simple microtasks, including quiz-style prompts, online surveys, and light research tasks you can complete from your mobile device or laptop.
You don’t need to go through a lengthy application process, and there’s no upfront cost. You can start earning quickly, often receiving your payouts in crypto or PayPal cash, depending on the task. This setup makes JumpTask perfect for taking surveys and other simple tasks in your spare time.
You can explore all available microtasks to start earning. It's worth trying for anyone looking for a flexible, low-pressure way to earn money by answering
questions.

Got answers? Get paid

From quick polls to survey questions, JumpTask helps you turn downtime into payouts.

2. JustAnswer

JustAnswer is another platform where experts can get paid to answer questions in fields like tech support, law, medicine, and more. This is a smart way to earn a flexible side income online if you have professional expertise and deep knowledge.
To get started:
  • Pass a background check and verify your credentials
  • Choose your specialty and set your rates
  • Start answering questions in your own hours
Top contributors can earn money consistently by offering informative answers and maintaining high ratings. Your earnings grow with each quality response, making it ideal for building steady, flexible income over time.
If you can deliver thorough answers and enjoy helping others, JustAnswer can be a rewarding way to make money answering questions from people who genuinely need expert help.

3. Studypool

Studypool lets you get paid to answer questions from students who need homework help across subjects like math, computer science, and literature. It’s a go-to for anyone with academic skills looking for extra money and flexibility.
Here’s how it works:
  • Browse posted academic questions across subjects like computer science, math, business, and writing
  • Place a bid based on your own rate and expected completion time
  • Once selected, submit thorough answers by the deadline
  • Get paid once your response is approved
Top tutors often earn money quickly by focusing on clarity, speed, and accuracy. Students rate your work, so giving informative answers boosts your reputation and chances of winning more bids.
If you enjoy helping students and want to make money answering questions on your own terms, Studypool offers a flexible, rewarding way to do it.

4. PrestoExperts

PrestoExperts allows users to register as online consultants and get paid to answer questions as they go. Categories range from business and education to health and tech, making it a versatile space for those with deep knowledge or specialized skills.
Key features include:
  • Live chat and call options for real-time support
  • Flexible scheduling so you can work around your own hours
  • Ability to set your own rate based on expertise
You earn per minute during calls or chats, and maintaining a high user rating helps attract extra cash by answering questions. The platform suits those who prefer a direct, conversational format and want to offer informative answers in their field.
If you have a strong grasp of high-income skills, this platform is a great way to turn your knowledge into a flexible income stream.

5. Quora+ (partner program)

Quora+ lets you earn based on the question-answer platform model, where your responses generate money based on interaction. Simply put, you earn based on your content's engagement, including views, reading time, and how many people interact with your answers.
To do well on the platform:
  • Focus on giving well-researched answers
  • Stick to topics where you can offer expert answers
  • Stay consistent to grow your presence and unlock more money
Quora+ works best for users who enjoy writing and can offer real value to others through detailed responses. Improving your engagement is key to boosting your reach. It’s an excellent platform for anyone looking to earn on their own schedule while helping others learn.

6. Fiverr (Q&A gigs)

Fiverr allows users to offer custom gigs focused on answering questions in specific areas, such as fitness, legal advice, or tech support. It works well for those with deep knowledge and the ability to explain things clearly.
Popular Q&A gig ideas include:
  • Answering health or nutrition questions for gym-goers
  • Helping small business owners with marketing questions
  • Providing tech advice for beginners
You can set your rates, build gig packages, and respond to questions at your own pace. Clear communication and quick delivery help build repeat clients and a strong reputation.
Many freelancers start with skills they already have and gradually expand. Strong engagement and clear value in your gig description can make a big difference. For those looking to turn expertise into flexible work, Fiverr is a great option that rewards consistency and clarity.

7. HelpOwl

HelpOwl is a knowledge-sharing platform where users get paid to answer questions by earning points. These points can be redeemed for gift cards and other rewards, making it a simple way to earn money during your free time.
To earn consistently:
  • Focus on detailed answers that genuinely help others
  • Write clearly and check grammar and formatting
  • Stay active to build trust and unlock higher reward tiers
Users who enjoy sharing knowledge on survey sites or contributing to paid survey sites may also find HelpOwl’s points system familiar. Though not a direct survey platform, it rewards effort through content rather than form-filling.
The platform values informative answers, so being thorough pays off. While the payouts aren’t instant, HelpOwl is a legitimate way to earn, especially for those who enjoy helping others and have a knack for writing well-researched answers.

8. Wonder

Wonder is a platform that hires researchers to answer questions that require in-depth, sourced information. Clients submit complex queries, and Wonder matches them with researchers who provide well-researched, professional responses.
To get started, users must:
  • Pass a brief application test that checks for grammar, logic, and research ability
  • Demonstrate strong writing skills and the ability to cite sources clearly
Wonder pays you per project, typically after you’ve answered a complex, research-heavy question. Because of the depth involved, you’ll often earn money by answering questions that are business-focused, academic, or based on market research. You’ll typically get paid to answer questions with payouts averaging between $8–$20 per completed query.
It’s ideal for subject matter experts or curious minds who enjoy diving into details. If you’re confident using a Google search to uncover verified insights and can manage your own hours, Wonder offers a flexible way to earn with each question answered.

9. LivePerson

LivePerson is a platform where professionals get paid to answer questions from customers through live chat. It’s ideal for those with deep knowledge in areas like tech, finance, health, or customer service.
You can create a profile and list your expertise. Once approved, you'll start getting connected with users seeking advice or information. Pay rates vary depending on your field and availability, but maintaining good response time and positive feedback helps increase your visibility and income potential.
Why it works well for experts:
  • Direct interaction with real customers
  • Option to choose your own hours and set availability
  • Earnings grow with experience and reputation
  • Real-time format makes it ideal for fast typers and multitaskers
It’s a solid option for anyone looking to earn money by offering expert answers without leaving home.

10. Brainly (Top Contributor Program)

Brainly is a popular platform where users answer questions to help students with homework and academic topics. While most contributors participate for free, top contributors can join Brainly’s special recognition programs and earn rewards based on helpfulness and consistency.
Answering frequently, offering detailed answers, and receiving upvotes can boost your profile. Brainly values clear explanations and accurate information, especially in subjects like math, science, and history.
Here’s how to build up on Brainly:
  • Focus on thorough answers with step-by-step explanations
  • Be consistent and stay active within your subject areas
  • Respond quickly to trending homework questions
  • Aim to help others while improving your own knowledge
As your reputation grows, so does your chance to earn extra rewards and gain community recognition. It’s a great option for those who enjoy teaching and want to support students in their free time.

Tips to earn more extra cash by answering questions online

  • Focus on your strengths: Stick to topics you know well. Platforms reward accurate answers and helpful feedback, especially in areas like tech, business, or education.
  • Join multiple platforms: Most platforms have varying payout terms and task types. By combining survey sites with Q&A sites, you can earn consistently while choosing what works best for your schedule.
  • Stay consistent: Answer regularly, even if it’s just a few questions a day. Over time, you’ll build trust and better ratings.
  • Look for fast-paying options: Some platforms offer quicker payouts or lower thresholds. If you're in a hurry to make money, check each site's payment terms carefully.
