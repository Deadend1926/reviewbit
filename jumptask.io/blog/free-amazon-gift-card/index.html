How to Get a Free Amazon Gift Card: 9 Best Ways

People are always looking for ways to save money. Some make weekly grocery purchases, others take up side hustles in their spare time, and others redeem free Amazon gift cards via several legitimate methods. 
It’s a great way to enjoy shopping without impacting your budget, so we’ll cover the best ways to get free Amazon gift cards.

1. Earn free gift cards on microtask platforms

Microtask platforms are great for people who want to complete small tasks and get Amazon gift cards. Such tasks range from watching videos and taking surveys to testing websites. 
One of the leading open engagement platforms that offer a variety of tasks, including paid surveys and tasks to watch videos, is JumpTask. By staying consistent with these microtasks, you can earn points and redeem Amazon gift cards for free.
You can easily claim gift cards by:
  • Dedicating time each day or week to complete microtasks.
  • Choosing specific tasks that help you claim free gift cards.
  • Monitoring your points and cashing out rewards so that you have a steady income in your bank account.

Free Amazon gift cards? Yes, please!

Complete simple tasks, cash out your earnings, and treat yourself to an Amazon gift card - or anything else you want.

2. Use cashback and receipt-scanning apps

Cashback and receipt-scanning apps let you earn cash for everyday purchases. You can easily scan your receipt or use a loyalty card when shopping to redeem points or cash from your favorite stores. 
Apps like Ibotta, Shopkick, and Rakuten let you redeem these rewards for Amazon gift cards.
To cash out an Amazon gift card, you’ll need to:
  1. Earn points or cashback by scanning receipts or making qualifying purchases.
  2. Check the minimum balance for redeeming a gift card on different apps. For example, Ibbota requires at least $20 before you can cash out. Fetch Rewards needs at least 3,000 points (equal to $3).
  3. Once you have the minimum balance, go to the rewards section in the app and select an Amazon gift card as your payout option.
  4. You’ll receive the gift card code in your email or app wallet.
  5. Copy the code and go to your Amazon account.
  6. Apply it to your balance under the option “Gift Cards”.
Make sure to scan receipts consistently to cash out gift cards faster.

3. Redeem credit card or loyalty program points

If you regularly shop online or travel, take full advantage of your credit card or loyalty programs to earn rewards. You’ll get perks and free stuff for your purchases as credit cards offer various rewards. 
Review your current credit cards and programs to see if they offer the option of redeeming free Amazon gift cards.
Once you earn points, you can turn them into e-gift cards by:
  • Logging into your account to see your balance.
  • Checking eligibility for free Amazon gift cards or other services.
  • Verifying participating retailers.
  • Selecting the desired reward.
Make sure to check the expiration dates on your earned points. You can see it in your bank account alongside other financial information. Otherwise, you’ll need to buy gift cards with your own money.

4. Participate in brand promotional giveaways

You can get Amazon gift cards by participating in specific giveaways. Brands like Too Faced Cosmetics, Men’s Health, Lego, and others do them periodically on social media channels, websites, or newsletters. 
Start by searching for your favorite brands on social media – both big and small names often host giveaways. Read through the instructions on how to enter and earn Amazon or other gift cards. 
Just remember – engage with trusted brands only to avoid any scams.

5. Sign up for online focus groups or product testing

Companies often reach out to customers for feedback by organizing online focus groups or product testing. You can get gift cards, cash via PayPal, or loyalty points for providing your opinion on their products or services.
For example, you can:
  1. Set up an Amazon account.
  2. Sign up to be a product tester – you won’t need to purchase any products as they’ll be sent to you.
  3. Spend time writing Amazon reviews.
  4. Earn Amazon gift cards.
Stay consistent and give thorough and honest feedback to claim gift cards faster.
Since you’ll be writing reviews, you’ll receive free products to test. It can be an alternative solution if you like to shop online as you won’t have to pay for anything.

6. Explore referral programs

Apps and websites offer referral programs where you can share your referral code with your friends, family, or social network and claim gift cards, redeem points, or get other perks.
For example, Vevox, an audience engagement platform, offers a $25 Amazon gift card for you and your referral once they’ve attended an online demo session. You’ll need to:
  1. Send an email with the invitation link to your friends and family.
  2. Make sure your referral attends the demo session.
  3. Get your referrals to purchase a Vevox plan and receive an additional $100 Amazon gift card.
You can find such information on Vevox’s dedicated referral page. However, pay close attention to the referral program guidelines – you can find them on the brand’s site. 
It’s important to remain transparent and ethical even when referring your loved ones.

7. Trade in old electronics or books

Amazon's Trade-In program allows you to exchange eligible electronics, books, and other items for rewards like Amazon gift cards. You can trade cell phones, tablets, and textbooks on this platform. Over 310 million users have an Amazon account and participate in such trades daily as you get free Amazon gift cards or real pay in return.
The Amazon website makes it super easy to list your items for sale:
  1. Choose your Amazon reward – your options range from Kindle discounts to free Amazon gift cards.
  2. Place a trade-in offer to sell your item by describing it and uploading pictures.
  3. Purchase a new device from Amazon.
  4. Ship your trade-in item to a designated Amazon location.

8. Play mobile games that offer rewards

Some games operators work with reward platforms to allow you to redeem Amazon gift cards while playing games. 
One of the most popular game platform is Freecash. It has a list of mini-games like Monopoly, Solitaire Grand Harvest, and Wheel of Fortune, so you can choose what interests you the most. 
Notably, each game has different rewards programs. You’ll need to check what’s available to you via Freecash’s dashboard.
Consistent gameplay will lead to better earnings, so spend time each week to reach these milestones. Additionally, you can get PayPal cash alongside Amazon gift cards.

9. Look for online “instant win” opportunities

Instant win games or sweepstakes offer quick chances to win prizes, earn points, and get Amazon gift cards or PayPal cash. These opportunities are typically luck-based, so it shouldn’t be your only option when looking for gift cards.
To find these opportunities, you can:
  • Explore discussions on Reddit
  • Search videos for guidance
  • Research platforms like Sweepstake Fanatics or The Freebie Guy
Reddit groups are especially useful. Keep an eye on new posts to stay ahead of the latest sweepstakes and snag a free Amazon gift card instantly, just for participating.

Key Takeaways

  • There are numerous legitimate ways to earn free Amazon gift cards, including microtask platforms, survey apps, cashback, and referral programs.
  • Always verify the legitimacy of platforms and promotions and search for discussion groups to see what other people are saying.
  • The best choice for you depends on how quickly you want to receive Amazon gift cards and how much time you can spend.
  • Explore multiple options and see which one fits your lifestyle to receive free gift cards.
