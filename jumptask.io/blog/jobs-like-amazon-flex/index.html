Jobs Like Amazon Flex: Top 8 Alternatives in 2026

December 22, 2025
Share:
If Amazon Flex no longer gives you the freedom or income you want, you are not alone. Many drivers look for delivery jobs like Amazon Flex that offer more flexibility, stronger earning potential, and less repetitive work. In this article, we’ll give you eight of the best alternatives that put you back in control and allow you to make money fast. 

1. Roadie

Roadie connects you with same-day deliveries that often involve oversized items, pickups from local businesses, or airport runs, which gives you more variety compared to delivering Amazon packages day after day. You browse available gigs in the app and accept only the ones that fit your schedule and your own vehicle.
Pay: Most Roadie drivers earn $10 to $40 per trip, depending on distance, item type, and urgency.

Want a break from delivery gigs?

Beyond Roadie and Amazon Flex, there are online tasks you can complete anytime.

2. GoShare

GoShare specializes in on-demand moving-style local deliveries, which can include couches, mattresses, office equipment, or store purchases. You see every job’s details before you accept it, including load size, distance, and pay, so you choose only the jobs your vehicle and schedule can handle. This can work well for independent contractors who want full control over their workload.
Pay: Helpers earn $20 to $35 per hour. Drivers with trucks or vans can earn extra money, $40 to $100 per hour.

3. Shipt

Shipt lets you shop for groceries and home goods, then deliver packages directly to customers. You manage each order from start to finish, which creates more interaction and tipping opportunities than standard package delivery.
Pay: Most Shipt shoppers earn $20 to $30 per hour, including tips when batching efficiently.

4. Instacart

Instacart app posts two types of jobs. You can shop and deliver full grocery orders or choose delivery-only batches where the store prepares everything. Once you log in, you can pick the type of work that fits your energy and schedule.
Pay: Drivers typically earn $18 to $28 per hour, including tips, with peak boosts available in busy cities.

5. DoorDash

DoorDash lets you deliver meals, groceries, and quick-stop items in nearly every market. You can start dashing instantly once your account is approved, pause whenever needed, and shape your workday around your availability instead of fixed shifts.
Pay: Dashers usually earn $15 to $30 per hour with tips and peak boosts depending on time, location, and how efficient you are.

6. Uber Eats

With Uber Eats, you handle food and quick shop deliveries from nearby merchants. You decide when to go online, when to take a break, and when to stop for the day, which makes it a great fit for flexible on-demand work.
Pay: Drivers typically earn $15 to $28 per hour, including tips, with surge pricing helping during busy periods.

7. Dolly

With Dolly, you take on moving style jobs such as carrying furniture, loading small loads, or helping customers relocate items within their homes. You pick the jobs you want and get paid for physical work that suits your skill level.
Pay: Helpers earn $20 to $35 per hour. Drivers with trucks earn $40 to $80 per hour.

8. TaskRabbit

With TaskRabbit, you get hired to handle everyday tasks like building furniture, doing light repairs, running errands, or helping with small moves. You set your rates and pick your hours, so you shape your workload the way you want.
Pay: Many Taskers earn $25 to $60 per hour, depending on the task, location, and experience.

Key takeaways

Here are the main points to remember when comparing alternatives to Amazon Flex:
  • You can earn more on tip-based platforms than on Amazon Flex when demand is high.
  • Food delivery and shopping apps offer more flexibility because you can go online anytime.
  • Moving and hauling apps pay the most but require more physical effort.
  • Some alternatives offer non-driving options if you want a break from the road.
  • You can improve your earnings by mixing several apps instead of relying on one.
